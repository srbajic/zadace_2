WHAT IS THE DEAD ANON POETS SOCIETY?

The dead anon poets society is an open poetry salon, in which every poet of
tumblr can submit their work anonymously, signing it with a pseudonym of
their choosing. There are two categories of poets in the society: the official
members and the guest members. Anyone can be published as a guest member.

"A few days ago, I received in my inbox an anonymous poem. A beautiful anonymous poem. It left me perplexed, but at the same time it amazed me beyond words. I really wished the anon-poet to go on with this poetry- reverie, so I encouraged her to absolutely do so (later i discovered it was the lovely @not-so-quiet-on-the-inside the one who started this wonderful project, almost as a game), but the way the all thing turned out left me in awe and I still cannot really believe this is not just a wonderful dream I'm about to wake up from: many other anon-poets began sending me their amazing words - and never stopped ever since.

So, as a joke, I started to call them “the dead anon poets society” and created a special space dedicated to them on my blog. As the number of poems increased, I promised I would officially found this society. So, with this, now I am. I think this to be a really wonderful opportunity for every poet here to get to share their talent with the world and certainly a magnificent idea which i think deserves a wider audience. So I tried to put all this into reality.."

LIS, the curator.
